Privacy Policy for Palper

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Ankush Gupta built the Palper app as a Freemium application. This service is provided by Ankush Gupta at no cost for the basic version and is intended for use as is.

Data Privacy & Offline Security

Palper is designed as an offline-first utility. Your passwords, notes, and credentials are stored exclusively on your device's internal storage. I do not have access to your data, and no data is transmitted to external servers.

Encryption and Storage

All sensitive information is protected using AES-256 encryption. The encryption keys are managed locally on your device using the Android Keystore system. Because I do not store your Master Password or encryption keys, I cannot recover your data if you lose your Master Password.

Information Collection and Use

For a better experience, while using our Service, I may require you to provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to local biometric authentication (fingerprint/face ID). The information that I request will be retained on your device and is not collected by me in any way.

Google Play Billing

Palper offers an optional Premium upgrade. Transactions are handled exclusively via the Google Play Billing system. I do not collect or store your credit card information or financial details.

Security Recommendations

Because your data is stored locally, its security depends on your device's security. We strongly recommend using a secure device lock (PIN, Pattern, or Biometrics) to prevent unauthorized access to the application.

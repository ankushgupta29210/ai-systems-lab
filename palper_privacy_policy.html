Privacy Policy for Palper

Effective Date: March 9, 2026

Ankush Gupta built the Palper app as a freemium application. This service is provided by Ankush Gupta at no cost for basic features and is intended for use as is.

Zero-Knowledge & Offline Storage

Palper is a fully offline application. Your passwords, credentials, and secure notes are stored completely locally on your device's internal storage. The developer does not have access to your data, and because the app operates entirely offline, no data ever leaves your device.

Encryption and Protection

All sensitive data within the app is protected using AES-256 encryption. Encryption keys are managed locally through the Android Keystore system. Because the developer does not store your Master Password or encryption keys on external servers, lost credentials cannot be recovered.

Information Collection and Use

No personal data, vault information, or device statistics are collected, transmitted, or stored on external servers.

The app may use your device's biometric authentication (such as fingerprint or face recognition) for secure access. This biometric data is handled entirely by the operating system and is never accessed or stored by the developer.

Payments and Billing

Palper offers an optional Premium upgrade processed through the secure payment infrastructure of Google Play. All financial transactions are handled by Google, and the developer does not collect or store payment information such as credit card or banking details.

Security

Your privacy and trust are important. Because your data is stored locally, the security of your information depends on the security of your device. It is strongly recommended to enable device security features such as a strong lock screen or biometric authentication.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Users are advised to review this page for any changes. Updates become effective immediately after being posted here.

Contact

If you have questions or suggestions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer.

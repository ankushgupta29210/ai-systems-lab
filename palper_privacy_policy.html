Privacy Policy – Palper

Palper is a secure vault application developed by Ankush Gupta.

Information Collection

Palper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Local Storage

All information stored inside Palper remains locally on the user's device.

Biometric Authentication

The application may use device biometric authentication (such as fingerprint or face unlock) provided by the operating system. This biometric data is handled entirely by the device and is never accessed or stored by the application.

Data Security

Palper is designed to store sensitive data securely on the device using encryption and secure storage mechanisms.

Third-Party Services

Palper does not share any information with third-party services.

Changes

This privacy policy may be updated in future versions of the application.

Contact

For questions regarding this policy you may contact the developer.

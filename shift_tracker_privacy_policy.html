Privacy Policy – Shift Tracker

Shift Tracker is a work-hour logging application developed by Ankush Gupta.

Information Collection

The app allows users to enter shift start time, end time, date and break duration.

Data Storage

All shift records are stored locally on the user's device.

No Data Collection

The application does not collect personal data, does not transmit information to servers, and does not track users.

Third-Party Services

The app does not share user data with third-party services.

Changes

This policy may be updated in future updates of the application.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this policy you may contact the developer.

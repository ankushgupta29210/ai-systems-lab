Futuristic Projects In Progress

Andhe Ki Lathi

While working and observing everyday life, I came across someone who could not see, but could hear perfectly. Watching how difficult it was for them to navigate simple environments made me think about a question:

What if a system could translate vision into sound in real time?

That moment led to the idea of Andhe Ki Lathi — an assistive AI-powered wearable device designed to help visually impaired individuals understand their surroundings through intelligent audio feedback.

The system uses computer vision and AI models to detect objects, describe scenes, and guide users safely through environments.

The first prototype is being developed as a compact wearable device that sits around the neck, continuously analyzing the environment and narrating important information to the user.

This project is currently in active development.
